Civil Engineering

Civil engineering invovles the designing and manufacturing of large scaled building and design projects as well as systems.
What do Civil Engineers do?
Chemical engineers typically do the following:
  • Manage budgets and project resources
  • Design and plan the layout of equipment
  • Liaise with clients and a variety of professionals including architects and subcontractors
  • assessing the sustainability and environmental impact of projects
  • Resolving design and development problems
On the whole Civil engineers conceive, design, build, supervise, operate, construct and maintain infrastructure projects and systems in the public and private sector

  • Arup - British multinational professional services firm headquartered in London which provides engineering, architecture, design, planning, project management and consulting services
